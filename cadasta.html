Cadasta

The Cadasta Foundation is working on a platform to collect, organize and document informal land rights. Through my work at SpatialDev, I worked on a small team to develop key components of the platform including a web map to visualize parcels, a portal to manage organizations, and a CMS to organize data on informal land rights. The project used Python, Javascript and Angular.
