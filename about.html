About

Hello, my name is Sarah! I am a software developer and urban planner living in the wonderful city of Seattle. For work, I build web-map applications to help NGOs and foundations visualize their spatial data. I enjoy working with clients to make their data accessible and understandable to a diverse audience. I primarily develop using Node.js, Angular.js, PostgreSQL, Leaflet, Grunt and HTML/Less.js. You can read about a couple of projects I have worked on here or here.

In my free time, I pursue projects which allow me to explore my interests in transportation, mobility, designing better cities, and DIY crafts. This site contains projects I have completed through work, as well as on my own. I am always looking for ways to collaborate with others, hone my creative skills, and use technology for social good so please say hello if you'd like to talk maps or work together on a project.
