About

Hello, my name is Sarah! I am a software engineer and urban planner living in the wonderful city of San Francisco. I work at Remix, where I get to build tools for transportation planners. I am currently working on a tool to help agencies better plan and schedule their bus routes so that they may provide higher quality service (at a lower price!) You can read more about the work going on at Remix here.

In my free time, I pursue projects which allow me to explore my interests in transportation, mobility, designing better cities, and DIY crafts. This site contains projects I have completed through work, as well as on my own. I am always looking for ways to collaborate with others, hone my creative skills, and use technology for social good so please say hello.
