Wanderlust

Friends of mine often ask for advice of what to do and see while traveling. I have been fortunate to visit a lot of great cities and wanted to organize my notes. So far, Google Maps has been the best solution I have found to tracking and mapping locations. Wanderlust lets me put all of my maps in the same place in a way that can be shared with friends and family.
