Lip Balm

My brother was nice enough to gift me ingredients for making lip balm. I found a million different recipes online and tried a bunch before finding a recipe I liked. My favorite mixture ended up being equal parts shea butter, beeswax and coconut oil. I also used a mixture of lavendar and sweet orange essential oils to add scent. I call my lip balm "Da Balm" because I think it rocks and I love bad puns.
